Exploring the cities I've visited in Russia

First city was : Moscow

Moscow is the capital city of Russia, is rich in history and culture. I lived in this city for 3.5 years. its a great city witch i liked very much.

Saint Petersburg

Saint Petersburg is known for its stunning architecture and cultural landmarks like the Winter Palace and the Hermitage Museum.

Kazan

Kazan is a beautiful city that blends Tatar and Russian cultures. The Kazan Kremlin is a must-see, with its unique mix of architectural styles.
